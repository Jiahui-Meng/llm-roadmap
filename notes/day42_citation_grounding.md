# Day 42 — Citation Grounding\n\n## Why grounding matters\nA RAG system is much more trustworthy when the answer is tied to evidence, not just plausible text.\n\n## What citation grounding does\n- attaches answer claims to source chunks\n- makes verification easier\n- exposes retrieval mistakes\n- reduces unsupported generation\n\n## Engineering meaning\nGrounding changes RAG from a convenience feature into a traceable system that can be audited and improved.\n\n## One-line summary\nCitation grounding turns retrieved context into accountable evidence, not just hidden prompt stuffing.\n
