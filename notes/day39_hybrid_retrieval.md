# Day 39 — Hybrid Retrieval\n\n## Why hybrid retrieval\nLexical retrieval is good at exact term matching. Dense retrieval is good at semantic similarity. Hybrid retrieval combines both, often giving a better balance of recall and precision.\n\n## Core intuition\n- lexical search catches exact keywords, numbers, names, and phrasing\n- dense search catches paraphrases and semantic matches\n- hybrid search reduces the blind spots of either method alone\n\n## Engineering meaning\nHybrid retrieval is often the practical default for production RAG because it is more robust across query types than pure dense retrieval.\n\n## One-line summary\nHybrid retrieval combines exact-match and semantic retrieval signals to improve robustness across diverse queries.\n
